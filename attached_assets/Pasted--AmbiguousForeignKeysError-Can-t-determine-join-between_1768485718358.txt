# AmbiguousForeignKeysError: Can't determine join between 'daily_question' and 'daily_question_response'; tables have more than one foreign key constraint relationship between them. Please specify the 'onclause' of this join explicitly.

**Issue ID:** 88568476
**Project:** python-flask
**Date:** 15/01/2026, 07:30:01
## Issue Summary
Ambiguous Join Error: SQLAlchemy needs explicit `onclause` for `daily_question` and `daily_question_response`.
**What's wrong:** AmbiguousForeignKeysError: **SQLAlchemy cannot determine join** between 'daily_question' and 'daily_question_response'. Multiple foreign keys exist, requiring explicit `onclause` specification.
**In the trace:** Multiple unrelated `psycopg2.OperationalError: SSL connection has been closed unexpectedly` errors occurred concurrently, suggesting potential database connectivity issues.
**Possible cause:** The `daily_question` table has **multiple foreign key columns** (`source_discussion_id`, `source_statement_id`, `source_trending_topic_id`) that could potentially join with other tables, leading to ambiguity when SQLAlchemy tries to infer joins with `daily_question_response`.

## Issue Summary

**In the trace:** The session is in a **PendingRollbackError** state due to a prior exception, requiring a `Session.rollback()` to proceed.
**Possible cause:** The pipeline may be attempting to **re-insert** a discussion with a pre-existing slug, possibly due to a race condition or a logic error in handling existing entries.

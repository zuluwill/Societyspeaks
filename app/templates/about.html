About Society Speaks

Participate in or start discussions that matter. Filter by your country, city, or topic and join the conversation.

Our Mission

Society Speaks was created to provide a platform where people can come together to discuss the most pressing issues facing society today. From local matters in your city to global challenges, we offer a space where every voice can be heard, and every opinion matters.

Why Use Society Speaks?

  • Engage in meaningful discussions about important topics that impact your community and beyond.
  • Share your thoughts, and see how your opinions compare to others globally.
  • Stay informed with insights from people around the world, all in one place.

Our Vision

We believe that open discussions can lead to a more connected, informed, and empathetic world. With the power of technology, Society Speaks aims to bridge the gap between people of different backgrounds and experiences, giving everyone the opportunity to contribute to the conversation. Find common ground or disagree agreeably.

How It Works

Simply choose a topic, join or start a discussion, and share your opinions. You can filter discussions by country, city, or topic, ensuring you engage with content that matters to you. Our integration with Pol.is ensures that your contributions are analyzed and displayed in a way that fosters deeper understanding and collaboration.

Join Us Today

Whether you're here to listen, learn, or lead, Society Speaks is the place to start the conversation. Join us today and make your voice heard!

Open Source & Community Driven

Society Speaks is proudly open source and community driven. We believe in transparency and collaboration, which is why our entire codebase is available on GitHub for anyone to explore, contribute to, or learn from.

Your sponsorship helps keep Society Speaks free, independent, and continuously improving. Every contribution supports our infrastructure costs and enables us to develop new features that empower meaningful public discourse.

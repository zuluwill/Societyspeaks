{{ _('👥 Identify Opinion Groups') }}

{{ _('Users are clustered based on voting similarity, not demographics. The system uses PCA (Principal Component Analysis) and Agglomerative Clustering to find natural groupings.')|safe }}

{{ _('✅ Detect Consensus Statements') }}

{{ _('Statements with ≥70%% overall agreement and ≥60%% in each group are marked as consensus.')|safe }}

{{ _('Statements with high agreement across all groups (≥65%% mean, low variance) that unite different perspectives.')|safe }}

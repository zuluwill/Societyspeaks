Managing Discussions

Learn how to monitor and maintain engaging discussions

Monitoring Participation

Keep track of your discussion's health and engagement:

  • Monitor participant count and engagement levels
  • Review new comments regularly
  • Observe opinion groups forming
  • Look for areas of consensus and division

Promoting Engagement

Tips for Maintaining Active Discussions:

  • Share your discussion on relevant platforms
  • Engage with participants' comments
  • Add new seed comments to explore emerging themes
  • Keep the conversation focused and constructive

Understanding Results

Interpreting Pol.is Visualizations:

Opinion Groups

  • Groups are formed automatically based on voting patterns
  • Each color represents a distinct opinion group
  • Size of groups indicates number of participants
  • Distance between groups shows opinion difference

Consensus Statements

  • Statements that most groups agree or disagree with
  • Useful for finding common ground
  • Can help bridge divides between groups

Participation Stats

  • Total number of participants
  • Number of votes cast
  • Number of comments submitted
  • Participation rates over time

Moderation Best Practices

Key Moderation Tasks:

  • Regularly review and moderate new comments
  • Maintain a respectful and constructive environment
  • Remove inappropriate or off-topic comments
  • Encourage balanced participation across groups

Setting Ground Rules:

  • Clear guidelines for participation
  • Expectations for comment quality
  • Consequences for rule violations
  • Process for appealing moderation decisions

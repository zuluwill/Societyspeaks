Frequently Asked Questions

When do I receive the brief?

You choose your preferred delivery time: 6am, 8am, or 6pm in your local timezone. The brief is generated daily around 5pm UTC and delivered when you want it.

What happens after the free trial?

After 14 days, you'll be invited to subscribe to keep receiving the brief. We're launching with a simple individual tier, with team plans coming soon. We'll notify you before any charges.

How do you decide what's in the brief?

Our system monitors trusted news sources and podcasts, scores articles for civic relevance (not just popularity), and filters out sensationalism. The final selection prioritizes stories that inform citizen understanding of important issues.

Is my data private?

Yes. We only store what's needed to deliver your brief (email, timezone, preferences). We don't sell your data or track your reading behavior. See our privacy policy for details.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. Every email includes a one-click unsubscribe link. No hoops, no guilt trips, no dark patterns.

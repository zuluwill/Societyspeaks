Common questions

Is this AI-generated?

Yes. We use AI to synthesise and summarise from your sources. Every claim links back to its source, and you can review and edit before sending.

How is this different from ChatGPT or Perplexity?

Those tools answer questions. We monitor sources and deliver briefings on a schedule. You're not searching — you're being briefed on what changed since yesterday.

Can I upload my own research documents?

Yes, on Professional and above. Upload PDFs and we'll synthesise them alongside your public sources. Great for combining internal research with external news.

Can I review before sending to my team?

Yes. Enable approval workflow and you'll receive a draft to review, edit, and approve before it goes out.

What if I don't like it?

Cancel anytime, no questions asked. 30-day free trial, no credit card required.

What sources can I add?

RSS feeds, Substack newsletters, web pages, and PDF documents. We support 60+ curated sources out of the box, or add your own custom sources.

How long does setup take?

About 2 minutes. Pick a template, customise your sources if you want, set your delivery time, and you're done. First brief arrives the next morning.

Do you offer team or enterprise trials?

Yes. Team and Enterprise plans include a 30-day free trial. For Enterprise, contact us to discuss your specific requirements and we'll set up a tailored pilot.

Can I keep my briefs private or share them publicly?

Both. Each brief can be private (just you), organisation-only (your team), or public (shareable link anyone can view). You control visibility per brief.

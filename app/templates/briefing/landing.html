Frequently Asked Questions

Is this generated?

Yes, but structured and auditable. Summaries are generated from your sources, and you can review and edit before sending.

Can I edit before sending?

Yes. You can choose fully automated delivery or require approval before each brief is sent.

Is Society Speaks still free?

Yes. Our public civic platform remains completely free and open source. This product is separate — premium briefing infrastructure for personal and professional use.

Do you sell data?

No. Your sources, your content, your data. We process and deliver — you own everything.

Can I keep things private?

Yes. Briefs are private by default. You can choose to share within your organisation or make them public, but privacy is the default.

How long does setup take?

About 5 minutes. Add your sources, choose a schedule, and you're done.

Can I change sources later?

Yes, anytime. Add or remove sources, change schedules, or pause your brief — all from your dashboard.

What if I don't like it?

Cancel anytime, no questions asked. Your data is yours — export it before you go, or we'll delete it on request.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this generated?

Yes, but structured and auditable. Summaries are generated from your sources, and you can review and edit before sending.

Should I start from a template or build my own?

Either works. Our Template Marketplace has 12 pre-built briefings covering topics like politics, tech, climate, and health — each with curated sources ready to go. Or start from scratch and pick your own sources. Templates can be fully customised after you create them.

Can I edit before sending?

Yes. You can choose fully automated delivery or require approval before each brief is sent.

Can I customise the AI writing style?

Yes. Choose from calm and balanced, formal, or conversational tones. You can also add custom instructions and enable guardrails to prevent sensationalist framing.

Can I send briefs to Slack?

Yes. Connect your Slack workspace and post briefs directly to any channel. Great for keeping teams aligned without adding to inbox clutter.

Is Society Speaks still free?

Yes, always. Our public civic platform remains completely free and open source. Personal Briefs is how we fund that work — subscriptions here directly support the open source platform, our algorithms, and keeping public discourse accessible to everyone.

Do you sell data?

No. Your sources, your content, your data. We process and deliver — you own everything.

Can I keep things private?

Yes. Briefs are private by default. You can choose to share within your organisation or make them public, but privacy is the default.

How long does setup take?

About 2 minutes if you use a template, or 5 minutes if building from scratch. Add your sources, choose a schedule, and you're done.

Can I change sources later?

Yes, anytime. Add or remove sources, change schedules, or pause your brief — all from your dashboard.

What if I don't like it?

Cancel anytime, no questions asked. Your data is yours — export it before you go, or we'll delete it on request.

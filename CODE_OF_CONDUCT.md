# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Society Speaks is committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in communication.
- Assume good intent and focus on constructive feedback.
- Welcome different viewpoints and experiences.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, threats, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Doxxing, intimidation, or sustained disruption
- Publishing private information without consent

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, and any community spaces associated with this project.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately following the process in `SECURITY.md` or by contacting the maintainer listed in `MAINTAINERS.md`.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, commits, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.
